About the College

R.K. Desai College, Vapi, established by the Ramanlal Kunvarji Desai Memorial Trust,
is one of the leading institutions of higher education in South Gujarat. The college was
founded with the vision of providing quality education to students from rural, industrial,
and urban areas, empowering them with knowledge and professional skills. Affiliated to
Veer Narmad South Gujarat University, Surat, the college offers diverse programs in
Commerce, Management, Computer Applications, Science, and Education. With modern
infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on academic as well as extracurricular
development, the institution continues to shape future professionals and responsible citizens.

Key Highlights

  • Established and managed by Ramanlal Kunvarji Desai Memorial Trust
  • Affiliated to Veer Narmad South Gujarat University (VNSGU), Surat
  • Undergraduate programs offered:
  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • B.Com (Gujarati & English Medium)
  • B.Sc. (Chemistry & Microbiology)
  • Dual medium of instruction in selected courses: Gujarati & English
  • Facilities: Spacious classrooms, modern laboratories, library, Wi-Fi-enabled computer labs, and student support services
  • Focus on holistic education through academics, cultural activities, workshops, and skill development initiatives
