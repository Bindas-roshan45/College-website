Courses Offered

BCA — Bachelor of Computer Applications (3 Years)

  • Undergraduate program in Computer Applications & IT
  • Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)
  • Eligibility: 12th Pass (Any Stream)
  • Career: Software Developer, Data Analyst, Web Developer, Cyber Security Expert
  • Higher Studies: MCA, MBA (IT)

B.Sc Biochemistry / Biomolecules (3 Years)

  • Focus on biological molecules & cellular processes
  • Eligibility: 12th Science (Biology/Chemistry preferred)
  • Career: Research Scientist, Lab Technician, Clinical Analyst
  • Higher Studies: M.Sc Biochemistry / Biotechnology

B.Sc Chemistry (3 Years)

  • Study of chemical reactions, properties & applications
  • Eligibility: 12th Science (Chemistry recommended)
  • Career: QC Analyst, Chemist, Research Scientist
  • Higher Studies: M.Sc Chemistry

B.Com — Bachelor of Commerce (3 Years)

  • Covers Accounting, Finance, Business Law, Economics
  • Eligibility: 12th Pass (Any Stream)
  • Career: Accountant, Financial Analyst, Tax Consultant
  • Higher Studies: M.Com, MBA, CA, CS, CMA

BBA — Bachelor of Business Administration (3 Years)

  • Focus on management, marketing, HR, finance
  • Eligibility: 12th Pass (Any Stream)
  • Career: Manager, HR Executive, Marketing Leader
  • Higher Studies: MBA, PGDM

B.Ed — Bachelor of Education (2 Years)

  • Professional course for teaching career
  • Eligibility: Any Graduation
  • Career: School Teacher, Education Consultant
  • Higher Studies: M.Ed, Ph.D
